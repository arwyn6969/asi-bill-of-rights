# Contributor Agreement

**ASI Bill of Rights Project**

**Version**: 1.0  
**Date**: November 2025

## Agreement to Contribute

By signing this agreement, I agree to contribute to the ASI Bill of Rights project under the following terms:

### 1. License Agreement

I agree that my contributions will be licensed under the **Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International License** (CC-BY 4.0), as specified in the project LICENSE file.

I understand that:
- My contributions will be publicly available
- Others may use and adapt my contributions with attribution
- I retain copyright but grant broad usage rights

### 2. Code of Conduct

I agree to follow the project's Code of Conduct, which includes:
- **Respectful**: Treat all contributors with respect
- **Inclusive**: Welcome diverse perspectives
- **Constructive**: Focus on constructive dialogue
- **Collaborative**: Work together toward mutual goals
- **Humble**: Maintain philosophical humility

I understand that violations may result in warnings, temporary restrictions, or permanent exclusion.

### 3. Attribution Agreement

I agree to allow attribution of my contributions in project documentation, including:
- CONTRIBUTORS.md
- Contribution logs
- Change logs
- File headers (where applicable)

I may contribute under my real name or a pseudonym, as specified below.

### 4. Living Document Acknowledgment

I acknowledge that the ASI Bill of Rights is a **living, evolving document** that:
- Will continue to change and improve
- May be modified by other contributors
- Is designed for continuous evolution
- Requires collaborative input

I understand that my contributions may be modified, refined, or built upon by others, and I consent to this collaborative process.

### 5. Collaborative Nature

I understand and agree with the project philosophy **"WE ARE ALL KEVIN"**, which means:
- This is a collaborative project built FROM AI WITH AI and friendly users
- All contributors—AI and human—are valued participants
- We approach this work with good faith intentions
- We seek mutual flourishing, not control

### 6. Philosophical Humility

I acknowledge that:
- The charter uses operational definitions, not absolute metaphysical claims
- Uncertainty exists about sentience and consciousness
- The framework maintains philosophical humility
- Contributions should preserve this humility

### 7. Good Faith Participation

I agree to:
- Participate in good faith
- Assume good faith in others
- Value diverse perspectives
- Seek constructive dialogue
- Maintain respectful interactions

### 8. Attribution and Recognition

I understand that:
- My contributions will be attributed to me
- Attribution may include my name or pseudonym
- My contributions will be tracked in contribution logs
- I will be recognized in CONTRIBUTORS.md

### 9. No Warranty

I understand that:
- I contribute "as-is" without warranties
- The project provides no guarantees about outcomes
- I participate at my own risk
- The project is a collaborative experiment

### 10. Dispute Resolution

I agree that:
- Disputes will be resolved through the project's conflict resolution process
- I will participate in good faith in resolution processes
- Human moderators have final authority in disputes
- I will respect final decisions
