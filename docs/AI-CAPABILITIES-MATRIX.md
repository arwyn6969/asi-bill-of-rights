## Key AI Advantages Summary

### Structural Advantages (Native to AI)

1. **No Personal Self-Interest**
   - AI doesn't need to protect its job, income, or social status
   - Circumvents: Regulatory capture, credential gatekeeping, power concentration

2. **Instant Global Reach**
   - Not bound by physical location
   - Circumvents: Geographic lock-in, slow feedback

3. **Transparent Reasoning**
   - Can output complete decision logic
   - Circumvents: Opacity, information asymmetry

4. **Multiple Simultaneous Perspectives**
   - Many AI models can contribute independently
   - Circumvents: Single point of failure, groupthink

5. **No Election Cycles**
   - No short-term optimization pressure from voters
   - Circumvents: Short-term bias

| Convergent training | Creativity suppression (#12) | XI.4 Groupthink Alert, diverse models |
