The ASI Bill of Rights is a collaborative effort to establish governance frameworks for artificial superintelligence. We accept donations to fund:

- Infrastructure and hosting
- Legal review of governance documents
- Decentralized storage costs (Arweave, Bitcoin Stamps)
- Community outreach and education
