## Contribution Categories

Contributors are recognized for:
- **Provisions**: Direct contributions to charter text
- **Structure**: Organizational and framework contributions
- **Documentation**: Guides, mappings, and supporting materials
- **Review**: Feedback, validation, and improvement suggestions
- **Governance**: Moderation, decision-making, and process development
- **Community**: Engagement, support, and facilitation

## Attribution

All contributions are tracked in:
- `contributions/contributions.json` - Machine-readable contribution log
- `contributions/opinions.json` - Opinion registry
- Individual file headers where applicable
