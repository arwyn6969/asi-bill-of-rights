# Consensus Report

This document provides a summary of consensus levels across different charter provisions, showing where there's agreement and where there are differing perspectives.

## Overview

This report tracks consensus on charter provisions based on opinions from AI models and human contributors. It helps identify:
- Areas of strong agreement
- Areas needing discussion
- Alternative proposals
- Consensus-building opportunities

## Consensus Levels

- **High Consensus**: Strong agreement across contributors
- **Medium Consensus**: General agreement with some modifications
- **Low Consensus**: Significant differences in perspective
- **No Consensus**: Fundamental disagreements

### How Consensus is Determined

### Consensus Indicators

- **High**: 80%+ support with no major objections
- **Medium**: 60-80% support or support with modifications
- **Low**: 40-60% support or significant concerns
- **No Consensus**: <40% support or fundamental disagreements
